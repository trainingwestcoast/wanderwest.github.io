    Mountain Lions


    This walk departs from the Greymouth Railway Station at 1pm each day. Pre-booking is essential to guarantee that a guide will be available.     Begin by walking across the Cobden Bridge for views of Mawheranui the Grey River, and the town of Greymouth.Then make your way up the Lions Walk to gain panoramic views of Mawheranui as it escapes out to sea. Total time 105 minutes including the return to the Railway Station.
     
    The climb up the Lions Walk is a short walk into the Rapahoe Range Scenic Reserve. You will climb steadily uphill through native bush-land before reaching a panoramic lookout with views of Greymouth the Tasman Sea and the Southern Alps. You will need good walking shoes and can expect muddy patches at some points, a reasonable level of fitness and agility is required.
     
    All tours include commentary and stories by professional storytellers.
